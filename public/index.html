About Us

Mission Statement:

At ICIC, we believe communication is a fundamental human right, not a privilege. Our mission is to bridge the gap between the deaf and mute community and the hearing world through innovative, accessible technology.

We are not just building tools; we are building an ecosystem. By combining wearable hardware with a comprehensive software learning platform, we aim to empower deaf children and adults to express themselves freely, learn effectively, and monitor their well-being‚Äîall without the need for bulky, intrusive equipment like sensory gloves. We are redefining accessibility, one gesture at a time.

Our Ecosystem

Our solution is divided into two integrated pillars: The Hardware (The Watch) and The Software (The Platform).

1. The ICIC Watch (Hardware)

Unlike traditional bulky sensory gloves, our device is a sleek, wrist-worn watch designed for comfort and everyday use.

Gesture-to-Speech Translation (Output):

Using advanced motion sensors, the watch detects sign language gestures and translates them instantly into spoken audio. This allows the user to "speak" to anyone, anywhere, breaking the silence barrier.

Speech-to-Visual Translation (Input):

Communication is a two-way street. When a hearing person speaks, the watch captures the audio and converts it into text or sign language animations on the display.

Supports: Arabic & English input ‚Üí Text or Sign Language output.

Health & Vitals Monitoring:

Integrated sensors monitor the user‚Äôs vital signs (heart rate, etc.) directly from the wrist. This data syncs seamlessly with our software platform, ensuring peace of mind for parents and caregivers. p>

2. The ICIC Platform (Software)

A web and mobile hub designed for education, translation, and health management.

The Learning Hub (Core Feature):

A gamified educational platform specifically designed for deaf and mute children. We focus on early childhood development, teaching them:

  • Sign Language: Mastery of gestures.
  • Written Languages: Arabic and English literacy.
  • Methodology: Interactive courses, quizzes, and visual engagement to make learning natural and fun.
Real-Time AI Translator:

A camera-based translation tool powered by computer vision.

  • ASL (American Sign Language): Fully supported real-time translation.
  • ESL (Egyptian Sign Language): Currently in active development. Note: Due to the scarcity of datasets for ESL, this feature is experimental, but we are actively gathering data to train our models for local context.
Health Dashboard:

A centralized profile that visualizes the biometric data collected by the ICIC Watch, allowing users or guardians to track health trends over time.
